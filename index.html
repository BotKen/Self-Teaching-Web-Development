TIM'S HTML

In the beginning,
Tim created the HyperText Markup Language.
The Internet was without form and void,
and text was upon the face of the monitor and
the Hands of Tim were moving
over the face of the keyboard.

And Tim said,
" Let there be links" ;
And there were links.
And Tim saw that the links were good;
And Tim separated the links from the text.

Tim called the links Anchors,
And the text He called Other Stuff.
And the whole thing together
Was the first Web Page.
